Shut The Box! is a classic pub game where players roll a pair of dice and remove tiles on the game tray that correspond with the numbers they roll. The goal of the game is to remove as many tiles as possible on a single turn before running out of options. Any tiles left upright at the end of a player's turn are added to their score.

The player with the lowest score wins!
